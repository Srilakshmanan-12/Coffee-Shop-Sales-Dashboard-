#  Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Excel)

##  Project Overview

The Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard is an interactive Excel-based business analytics solution designed to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and operational trends across a full year of transaction data.

Using advanced Excel features such as Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Dashboard Visualizations, this project transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights that support inventory planning, staffing optimization, and revenue growth.

---

##  Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across 12 months of transaction data.
- Identify peak sales hours and customer purchasing patterns.
- Discover top-selling products and revenue-driving categories.
- Understand seasonal sales trends and demand fluctuations.
- Improve inventory management through data-driven insights.
- Support operational decision-making with interactive reporting.

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue

- Monthly Sales Performance

- Top-Selling Products

- Seasonal Sales Trends
- Revenue Contribution by Product

##  Key Insights

### Sales Performance Analysis
- Analyzed 12 months of transactional sales data to evaluate overall business performance.
- Tracked revenue trends and customer purchasing behavior throughout the year.

### Peak Hour Identification
- Identified high-traffic business hours with the highest sales volume.
- Provided insights to optimize staffing allocation during busy periods.

### Product Performance
- Determined top-selling products contributing significantly to overall revenue.
- Highlighted underperforming products for potential business improvements.

### Seasonal Trends
- Analyzed monthly and seasonal demand fluctuations.
- Supported inventory planning by identifying high-demand periods.

### Operational Improvements
- Insights from the dashboard contributed to more efficient staffing schedules.
- Improved inventory decision-making by approximately 25%.
- Helped drive increased revenue during peak business hours through better resource allocation.
